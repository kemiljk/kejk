I'm Karl, a human-centred product and experience designer living in London, UK. Right now, I work at Aqovia.

I started out my career with a deep love of psychology and a Masters in Fine Art (New Media) creating behaviour-influencing sculptures and installations with digital components. Now, I lead a digital team to deliver elegant human-centric user experience solutions to complicated problems in the healthcare sector.

I regularly practice mindfulness and meditation, and I’m an art, design and architecture nerd that absolutely loves exploring and learning about how anything is built.

I've also worked for Cognite, Incuna and Apple.
