KEJK

I'm Karl, a human-centred designer and frontend developer. I lead design at NeuerEnergy in London, UK.

I started out my career with a deep love of psychology and a Masters in Fine Art creating behaviour-influencing sculptures and digital installations. Now, having spent 6 years bringing design thinking practices to rare diseases, I lead a design team delivering elegant human-centric solutions to change the face of renewable energy.

I regularly practice mindfulness and meditation, and I’m an art, design and architecture nerd that absolutely loves exploring and learning about how anything is built.

I've also worked for Cognite, Incuna and Apple.
