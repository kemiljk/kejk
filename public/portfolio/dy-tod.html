DUCHENNE AND YOU / TAKE ON DUCHENNE

WHO WAS IT FOR?

Duchenne is an extremely rare condition affecting around 1 in 3,500 children. It is due to a defective chromosome that stops proteins from being created correctly.

Resources for parents and healthcare professionals are hard to come by when it comes to rare disease and our client saw a need for providing a wealth of information to both audiences.

WHAT CHALLENGES DID THEY HAVE?

One of the biggest factors we were made aware of through parent interviews was the rarity of the disease and the likelihood that a child didn’t have Duchenne. The worry, is that it’s easy as a frightened parent of a young child displaying normal symptoms of slow development to see Duchenne as a possible answer and be terrified at seeing their child will die young. This potential scaremongering is something our client wished to avoid at all costs.

Healthcare professionals on the other hand struggled with a lack of high-science information for them to read and understand. A GP will likely never see a case in their lifetime but if they did, they’d need ample resource to help them.

WHAT MIGHT THEY NEED?

The conversations led us to believe that a platform needed to exist online with parent and HCP-friendly information on it. It needed to focus heavily on showing only the most important and relevant information to someone when they needed it.

WHAT DID WE DO?

The outcome was a pair of websites that had a share information architecture and interface paradigm. They leveraged a collapsed nav by default at all screen sizes (to reduce the urge to skip to the “worst case scenario”), deeper content hidden behind pop ups that are also bullet points and section footers that encouraged moving to the next section when they’d read the whole site (thus confirming the content was still relevant).

Platform

HTML, CSS, JavaScript and PHP hosted through Wordpress.

Rollout

Duchenne and You is currently live in Europe, the US, Spain, Germany and Portugal. Take on Duchenne is currently live in Europe.
